About Us

Moksha is a Hindu Malayali cultural organization devoted to preserving our heritage while embracing the diversity of Canadian society. We believe in nurturing identity, unity, and belonging for future generations.

Mission

Moksha is dedicated to preserving and promoting Hindu Malayali culture, rooted in the timeless values of"Sanatana Dharma". Through cultural education, community engagement, and shared experiences, we aim to pass on our traditions, language, and values to future generations while nurturing a spirit of unity and belonging in Canadian society.

Vision

To build a vibrant, inclusive community where the wisdom of the Upanishads — “Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam” (The World is One Family) — guides us in creating a multicultural and harmonious society for future generations.
