About Us

Moksha is a hindu malayali cultural organization promoting Hindu Malayali culture among younger generations. We aim to pass on our traditions, values, language, and heritage through meaningful cultural experiences and community engagement.

Our Programs

Cultural Events

Celebrate festivals like Onam, Vishu, and temple traditions with families and children.

Heritage Learning

Interactive sessions on rituals, values, and the philosophy of our culture.
